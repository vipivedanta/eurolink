About Us

A Kerala Based Overseas Education consultant. Our expertise is in providing professional services to the students seeking International Education.
We are the authorized agent for The Learning Works Malta. Learning Works is a subsidiary of Vassallo group, one of the largest employers in Malta in Private sector. We offer different courses in various sectors such as Hospitality, Management, Computer science and Languages. The courses offered by Learning Works will definitely help the students to become employable in the European Market and to enhance their Career advancements.
